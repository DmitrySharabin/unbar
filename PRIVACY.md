# Unbar — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 8, 2026

## Summary

Unbar does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

## What Unbar does

Unbar moves your current browser tab into a popup window without an address bar. The operation happens entirely within your local browser using the Chrome Extensions API (`chrome.windows.create`). Your saved window-size presets are stored locally on your device via `chrome.storage.local` and never leave it.

## Data collection

Unbar collects no data of any kind:

- No browsing history
- No page content
- No personal information
- No analytics or telemetry
- No cookies
- No network requests

## Permissions

- **activeTab** — used to identify the current tab so it can be moved into a new window. Access is granted only when you click the extension icon or trigger its keyboard shortcut, and is limited to that tab.
- **storage** — used to save your window-size presets locally on your device.

## Third parties

Unbar does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the update will be posted here. Since Unbar collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
